NOROT.

Stop rotting. Start living.

In an era of infinite scrolls and addictive algorithms, Norot is the digital antidote. It’s not just an "app blocker"—it’s a high-performance, discipline-first companion designed to break the cycle of mindless scrolling and turn your phone back into a tool of intent.

Mindful Interception

Predictive strike aborts app launches before they open. It doesn't just stop you; it asks: "Do you really need this right now?"

Tactical Allowances

Set a specific 1-15 min allowance using a premium, haptic-enabled time picker. Wait for the cooldown timer before entering. High-friction prevention.

Pure Black Privacy

Recents Blackout entirely hides restricted apps in the task switcher. No peeking. Protected by FLAG_SECURE to prevent screenshots.
